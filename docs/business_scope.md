## Objective
Build an end-to-end analytics platform to analyze healthcare operations and revenue cycle performance using synthetic and public data.

## Business Processes Covered
- Patient appointments and visits
- Insurance claims and payments
- Claim denials and revenue leakage
- Operational metrics (no-shows, visit volume)

## Key Metrics
- Total billed vs paid revenue
- Claim denial rate
- Days in Accounts Receivable (A/R)
- Revenue by department and payer
- Appointment no-show rate

## Target Users
- Healthcare operations managers
- Revenue cycle analysts
- Finance and analytics teams
